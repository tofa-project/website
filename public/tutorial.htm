Tutorial

1. First download Tofa from here. Upon launching it you will be greeted with this screen.

It's important to choose a strong password and remember it forever! This password is used to encrypt/decrypt your local database which holds your onion services private keys. If you ever loose your password you will never be able to use the 2FA features for the apps you have created. Database is encrypted using AES-256-GCM with random salts on each write.

2. After choosing the password you will be greeted with the following screen. To create your first ever app click on the button:

Name must be unique for each app, but that will be checked by Tofa Client. After creation, your app will be broadcasted to the Tor network, process which will take several seconds.

3. "Published" indicates if app is published to Tor network. "Registered" indicates if app is registered with the online service you're going to link it to. As end user, this should be enough to bother with! To see the Tofa URI you need to supply to the online service hit the "view button" correlated with your app.

Excellent! You can create more apps and register them with different services at your will. Remember to keep Tofa Client open whenever you wish to use 2FA with the registered services. And never never post information about your app anywhere else to avoid privacy disclosure and impersonation, which can occur if you share your app ID.
